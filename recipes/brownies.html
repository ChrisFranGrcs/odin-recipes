Brownies Recipe

Description

Chocolate brownies dessert recipe. Recipe taken from allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder

Frosting:

  • 3 tablespoons of butter, softened
  • 3 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of confectioner's sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F(175°C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and a teaspoon of vanilla. Beat in the cocoa, flour, salt and baking powder. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
  4. To make frosting: Combine 3 tablespoons of softened butter, cocoa, honey, vanilla extract, confectioner's sugar. Stir until smooth. Frost the brownies while they are still warm.
